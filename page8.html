Founder's Message

The JEXTOBAN project was conceived at a time when old values we once cherished were fast disappearing in the nation’s educational system. High failure rates with mass examination malpractices were rampant. The learning process in schools was a far cry from what we enjoyed in the late fifties and early sixties when the teachers were very committed and facilities adequately available. To ameliorate these lapses in our own little corner, JEXTOBAN was conceived and brought alive.

Our mission therefore is “to develop the total personality of the JEXTOBAN child through Christian virtues and values to excel in the global society”. We are therefore cut out for Qualitative Education and Good Character.

We endeavor to impart into our students such Christian virtues of transparent honesty, diligence, moral rectitude and of course the fear of God which all translate into excellence in academics and morals.

We are happy to note that we have witnessed a steady realization of our dreams, hopes and aspirations since our founding. A high percentage of our students are able to transit into institutions of higher learning the same year they pass out from us, due to the solid foundation laid for them here in JEXTOBAN.

We can joyfully report that very many of our students have come out with first class honors, or second class upper grades in their first degree results from different Nigeria and Overseas Universities like University of Ibadan, Obafemi Awolowo University, University of Lagos, Covenant University, Redeemer University of Nigeria, University of Aston, Bells University, and University of Nigeria to mention but a few. Today we have every reason to celebrate our students as they excel in their chosen professional fields as a result of the values instilled into them.

In the 2014/2015 academic session, we opened the permanent site at Km 36, Lagos-Ibadan Express Way, in OGUN State.

Our testimonies anchor on parents who had almost lost hope on their wards before they brought them to JEXTOBAN but who are today rejoicing that they made the right choice for their wards as such children have been completely transformed.

I like to WELCOME YOU ABOARD the JEXTOBAN FAMILY where all stakeholders work together in harmony sharing common hopes and aspirations in building the future generation of leaders .

Pastor E. A. OJO & Mrs. A. A. OJO
